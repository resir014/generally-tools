Downforce reference table.

Downforce Type of car
>30 Most mass-produced production cars, also most historic race cars without wings.
25 - 100 Low tech touring and GT race or tuned cars.
100 - 190 Low tech open-wheel cars or medium tech touring or GT cars.
180 - 250 High tech GT and touring cars or medium tech open-wheel cars.
250+ Le Mans Prototypes are at the lower end. Most of the very fast open-wheel cars (anything roughly equal to or faster than a Formula Renault 3.5) are probably good for 300+. Consider 400 the upper limit for anything.
