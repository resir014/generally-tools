Downforce reference table.

Downforce Type of car
0 Most production cars.
5-15 Common, low-range sports cars with spoilers.
15-30 Mid-range sports cars with spoilers and/or undertray diffusers.
30-80 Moderately modified versions of the low-end sports cars, and supercars.
70-115 Ultimate track-day oriented supercars and racing-type touring cars.
100-140 FIA and ACO based GT2 and GT3 racing cars, and FIA's WRC rally cars.
130-150 FIA and ACO based GT1 racing cars from after 2000.
150-165 All SuperGT-based GT300 racing cars and all DTM post-2000 and pre-1994 cars.
155-175 All SuperGT-based GT500 racing cars and all DTM cars from 1994-1999.
170-190 ACO based LMP (any class) cars.
180-200 All FIA based Group C (any class) cars.
200+ Modern F1, Indy racecars.
